The Gentlemen (The Gentlemen: The lords of the mafia in Spain) is a 2019 British crime and action film directed, written and produced by Guy Ritchie, and co-written with Ivan Atkinson and Marn Davies. It stars Charlie Hunnam, Henry Golding, Michelle Dockery, Colin Farrell, Hugh Grant, and Matthew McConaughey.


Mickey Pearson is an American expat who got rich building a marijuana empire in London. When word spreads that he is looking to get money out of the business, it soon unleashes a series of plots and plans from those who want his fortune.
